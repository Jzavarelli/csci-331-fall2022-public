Brother's of Metal Rocking Fan Page

The Brother's of Metal are a norse metal band that create mythological music. The band focuses on music that is modern and well versed within the metal genre.

The band is amazing and does not lean into the screaming portion of metal music. I enjoy their songs and would listen to certain albums for hours on end. You can listen to their music on their Webpage.
